A. P. J. ABDUL KALAM

1931 - 2015

missile man of india

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India," rose from humble beginnings to become a visionary scientist and the 11th President of India. His leadership of the Integrated Guided Missile Development Program (IGMDP) resulted in the development of indigenous missile systems, showcasing India's technological prowess. Beyond his official duties, Kalam's humility, accessibility, and dedication to inspiring youth made him a beloved figure nationwide. His tragic passing in 2015 left a void, but his legacy of perseverance, innovation, and service continues to inspire millions, solidifying his place as a beacon of hope and inspiration for generations to come.
